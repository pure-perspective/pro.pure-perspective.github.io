Maxwell's equations

在這一部分，我們在給定Source下，討論EM Field的分佈（給定Source下表示我們不對Source下的分佈\(\eta ^\mu\) 作變分）。 $$S_{PF}+S_F=\int - ho_m c-{1\over c^2} A_\mu J^\mu d^4 +\int-{1 \over 16\pi c} F_{\mu u} F^{\mu u} d^4 x$$ 我們想知道EM Field的分佈，所以我們針對\(A^\mu\) 作變分 $$A^\mu o A^\mu +\delta A^\mu $$ 我們來觀察\(J^\mu \)、\(d^4 x\)、\(F^{\mu u}\) 會不會受到影響？

$$J^\mu = ho_e {dx^\mu \over d au} =J^\mu (x^ u )$$ $$d^4 x=d^4 x(x^ u )$$ $$F^{\mu u} =\partial ^\mu A^ u -\partial ^ u A^\mu =F^{\mu u} \left(A^\omega ight)$$ 可見只有電磁張量\(F^{\mu u}\) 會受到\(A^\mu \)的變分\(A^\mu o A^\mu +\delta A^\mu\) 有關 $$F^{\mu u} \left(A^\omega ight) o F^{\mu u} (A^\omega +\delta A^\omega )=F^{\mu u} \left(A^\omega ight)+\delta F^{\mu u} $$ 所以\(S_{PF}\)的變分很簡單 $$\delta S_{PF}=\delta \int -{1 \over c^2} A_\mu J^\mu d^4 x$$ $$ =-{1 \over c^2} \int \delta A_\mu \cdot J^\mu d^4 x $$ 至於\(S_F\)的變分就稍嫌複雜 $$S_F=-{1 \over 16 \pi c} \int F_{\mu u} F^{\mu u} d^4 x$$ $$\delta S_F=-{1 \over 16 \pi c} \delta \int F_{\mu u} F^{\mu u} d^4 x =-{1 \over 16 \pi c} \int \delta F_{\mu u} \cdot F^{\mu u} +F_{\mu u} \cdot \delta F ^{\mu u} d^4 x $$ 利用Thm.4: 對Scalar變分與上下標無關，所以\(\delta F_{\mu u} \cdot F^{\mu u} =F_{\mu u} \cdot \delta F ^{\mu u}\) ，會有兩倍 $$\delta S_F=-{1 \over 16 \pi c} \int 2\delta F_{\mu u} \cdot F^{\mu u} d^4 x =-{1 \over 8 \pi c} \int \delta F_{\mu u} \cdot F^{\mu u} d^4 x $$ $$=-{1 \over 8 \pi c} \int \delta (\partial _\mu A_ u -\partial _ u A_\mu )\cdot F^{\mu u} d^4 x $$ $$=-{1 \over 8 \pi c} \int \delta (\partial _\mu A_ u )\cdot F^{\mu u} d^4 x +{1 \over 8 \pi c} \int \delta (\partial _ u A_\mu )\cdot F^{\mu u} d^4 x $$ 因為每一項\(\mu\) 、\( u\) 都是Dummy index，可以互換\(\mu \leftrightarrow u \)，我們把第一項的\(\mu\) 、\( u\) 互換，就可以把兩項合併 $$\delta S_F=-{1 \over 8 \pi c} \int \delta (\partial _\color{red}{ u} A_\color{red}{\mu} )\cdot F^\color{red}{ u \mu} d^4 x +{1 \over 8 \pi c} \int \delta (\partial _ u A_\mu )\cdot F^{\mu u} d^4 x $$ $$={1 \over 8 \pi c} \int \delta (\partial _ u A_\mu )\cdot (-F^ u \mu +F^{\mu u} ) d^4 x $$ 回憶電磁張量 $$F^{\mu u} =\partial ^\mu A^ u -\partial ^ u A^\mu =\left(egin{matrix}0 & -E_x & -E_y & -E_z\ E_x & 0 & -B_z & B_y \
