Charge Particle與EM Field作用下的變分與運動方程

在這一部分中，我們想探討一個Charge Particle在給定的EM Field下如何運動？（注意喔，給定的EM Field表示我們不對EM Field作變分）。相對論性電磁學下我們會寫下4-Potential \(A= A^\mu \hat{e} _\mu =\left(\phi,\overrightarrow{A} ight)\)，在這邊採用高斯制（Gaussian unit），而Charge Particle交互作用的Action \(S_{PF}\)會寫成 $$S_{PF}=\int _a^b -{e \over c} A_\mu d\eta ^\mu $$ 完整的描述Charge Particle運動即為 $$S=S_P+S_{PF}=\int _a^b -mc^2 d au +\int _a^b -{e\over c} A_\mu d\eta ^\mu $$ 在這邊，我們考慮Charge Particle在時空中的路徑作\(\eta ^\mu\) 變分 $$\eta ^\mu o \eta ^\mu +\delta \eta ^\mu $$ 雖然我們沒有對EM Field \(A^\mu\) 作變分，但是走不同路徑感受到的位能是不一樣的，所以Action走不同的路徑會有不同的\(A^\mu \)（意思是\(A^\mu \) 的變化來自於路徑 \(\eta ^\mu\) 不同，而不是對\(A^\mu\) 作變分） $$A^\mu (\eta ^\mu ) o A^\mu (\eta ^\mu +\delta \eta ^\mu )=A^\mu (\eta ^\mu )+\delta A^\mu $$

我們計算\(\delta S_{PF}\)如何變分： $$\delta S_{PF}=\delta \int _a^b -{e\over c} A_\mu d\eta ^\mu $$ $$ =-{e\over c} \int _a^b \delta A_\mu \cdot d\eta ^\mu -{e\over c} \int _a^b A_\mu \cdot \delta d\eta ^\mu $$ 利用Thm.1的方法，我們將\(\delta d\eta ^\mu \)對調\(d\delta \eta ^\mu \) $$\delta d\eta ^\mu =d\delta \eta ^\mu $$ 並作分部積分，邊界項會消失 $$\delta S_{PF}=-{e\over c} \int _a^b \delta A_\mu \cdot d\eta ^\mu -{e\over c} \int _a^b A_\mu \cdot d\delta \eta ^\mu $$ $$ =-{e\over c} \int _a^b \delta A_\mu \cdot d\eta ^\mu \color{red}{ -\left[{e\over c} A_\mu \cdot \delta \eta ^\mu ight] \Big|_a^b}+{e\over c} \int _a^b dA _\mu \cdot \delta \eta ^\mu $$ 利用Thm.3同乘同除\(d au \)不影響變分 $$\delta S_{PF}=-{e\over c} \int _a^b \delta A_\mu \cdot d\eta ^\mu +{e\over c} \int _a^b dA _\mu \cdot \delta \eta ^\mu $$ $$ =-{e\over c} \int _a^b \delta A_\mu \cdot {d\eta ^\mu \over \color{red}{d au}} \color{red}{d au} +{e\over c} \int _a^b {dA _\mu \over \color{red}{d au}} \color{red}{d au} \cdot \delta \eta ^\mu $$ 因為 $$\delta A_\mu ={\partial A_\mu \over \partial x ^ u } \delta \eta ^ u $$
$${dA_\mu \over d au} ={\partial A_\mu \over \partial x^ u } {d\eta ^ u \over d au} ={\partial A_\mu \over \partial x^ u } U^ u $$ 代入得到 $$\delta S_{PF}=-{e\over c} \int _a^b {\partial A_\mu \over\partial x^ u } \delta \eta ^ u \cdot u^\mu d au +{e\over c} \int _a^b {\partial A_\mu \over \partial x^ u } U^ u d au \cdot \delta \eta ^\mu$$ $$=-{e\over c} \int _a^b (\partial _ u A_\mu ) u^\mu \delta \eta ^ u d au +{e\over c} \int _a^b \left(\partial _ u A_\mu ight) U^ u \delta \eta ^\mu d au $$ 我們想要把變分\(\delta \eta ^ u \)和\(\delta \eta ^\mu\) 一起提出來，但是上標不一樣。但因為每一項\(\mu\) 、\( u\) 都是Dummy index，可以互換\(\mu \leftrightarrow u\) ，我們把第一項的\(\mu\) 、\( u\)互換，就可以把兩項合併 $$\delta S_{PF}=-{e\over c} \int _a^b \left(\partial _\color{red}{\mu} A_\color{red}{ u} ight) U^\color{red}{ u} \delta \eta ^\color{red}{\mu} d au +{e\over c} \int _a^b (\partial _ u A_\mu ) U^ u \delta \eta ^\mu d au $$ $$=-{e\over c} \int _a^b (\partial _\mu A_ u -\partial _ u A_\mu ) U^ u \delta \eta ^\mu d au $$ 完整考慮Charge Particle在EM Field中的運動 $$\delta S=\delta S_P+\delta S_{PF}=0$$ 所以 $$\delta S_P+\delta S_{PF}=\int _a^b m {dU_\mu \over d au} \delta \eta ^\mu d au -{e\over c} \int _a^b \left(\partial _\mu A_ u -\partial _ u A_\mu ight) U^ u \delta \eta ^\mu d au $$ $$=\int _a^b \left[m {dU_\mu \over d au} -{e\over c} \left(\partial _\mu A_ u -\partial _ u A_\mu ight) U^ u ight] \delta \eta ^\mu d au =0$$ 會得到 $$m {dU_\mu \over d au} -{e\over c} \left(\partial _\mu A_ u -\partial _ u A_\mu ight) U^ u =0$$
$$m {dU_\mu \over d au} ={e\over c} \left(\partial _\mu A_ u -\partial _ u A_\mu ight) U^ u \equiv {e\over c} F_{\mu u} U^ u $$ 我們定義電磁張量Electromagnetic Tensor $$F_{\mu u} =\partial _\mu A_ u -\partial _ u A_\mu $$ 電場\(\overrightarrow{E}\)、磁場\(\overrightarrow{B}\)與\(\phi\)、\(\overrightarrow{A}\)的關係（高斯制） $$ \overrightarrow{E}=- abla \phi-{1\over c} {\partial \overrightarrow{A}\over \partial t}$$ $$\overrightarrow{B}= abla imes \overrightarrow{A} $$ 其中 $$A_ u o \left(\phi,-A_x,-A_y,-A_z ight)$$ $$\partial _\mu o \left({\partial \over \partial x^0},{\partial \over \partial x^1 },{\partial \over \partial x^2},{\partial \over \partial x^3 } ight)=\left({\partial \over \partial ct},{\partial \over \partial x },{\partial \over \partial y},{\partial \over \partial z } ight)$$ 可以計算 $$F_{\mu u} =\partial _\mu A_ u -\partial _ u A_\mu =\left(egin{matrix}0 & E_x & E_y & E_z\ -E_x & 0 & -B_z & B_y \ -E_y & B_z & 0 & -B_x \ -E_z & -B_y & B_x & 0 \end{matrix} ight)$$ $$F^{\mu u} =\partial ^\mu A^ u -\partial ^ u A^\mu =\left(egin{matrix}0 & -E_x & -E_y & -E_z\ E_x & 0 & -B_z & B_y \ E_y & B_z & 0 & -B_x \ E_z & -B_y & B_x & 0 \end{matrix} ight)$$
