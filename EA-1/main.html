â–²The Derivation of the 4-Point Probe Method. \(R_c\) represents the contact resistance (\( \sim 10\Omega \)), and \(R_V\) is the internal resistance of the voltmeter (\( > 10M\Omega \)), such that \(R_V >> R_c\). For small resistance and superconductivity measurements, the contact resistance \(R_c\) becomes significant, making the traditional 2-point probe method inaccurate. The 4-point probe method eliminates the contribution of the contact resistance \(R_c\). (Click to enlarge)

LabVIEW Design

LabVIEW codes control and collect information from the instruments through GPIB. Instruments include Keithley 237 and 617 meters, HP34420A meter, Stanford Research Systems SR830 Lock-in, and Cryo-Con 32B temperature controller.The original LabVIEW code was not modularized, making it difficult to expand. After modularization, the program became scalable to multiple measurement loops.
